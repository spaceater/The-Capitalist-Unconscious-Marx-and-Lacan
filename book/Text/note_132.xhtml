这是拉康从阿尔都塞对《资本论》的解读中采用的：“无论他的评论家们是否为结构主义者，他们似乎已经表明马克思本人是一个结构主义者。因为它完全处于这样一点上……这是由劳动力市场的主导地位决定的，而剩余价值的功能被揭示为马克思“思想”的原因”（同上，17）。
This is what Lacan adopted from Althusser’s reading of Capital: ‘Whether his commentators are structuralist or not, they seem to have demonstrated that Marx himself is one, namely a structuralist. For it is properly by being at the point . . . that is determined by the predomination of the labour market, that the function of surplus-value is revealed as the cause of Marx’s thought’ (Ibid., 17).
