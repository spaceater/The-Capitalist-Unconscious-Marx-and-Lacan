同样的结论也适用于商品的分销、流通和消费。然而，马克思在《资本论》中对使用价值的中肯分析却无疑表明，即使是最遥远、最私人的消费行为，也不能免除剩余价值的提取和资本的规则。
The same conclusion is applicable to the distribution, circulation and consumption of commodities. Marx's moderate analyses of use-value in Capital nevertheless leave no doubt that even the most remote and private act of consumption is not exempt from the extraction of surplus-value and from the imperatives of capital.
