回想一下拉康在《研讨班7》中的立场，在这次研讨班中，这个词以黑格尔的方式被理解为对大写之物的谋杀，是对享乐的禁欲的能指。这个主题是源于关于弗洛伊德的主题，即在《图腾与禁忌》中对原始父亲的谋杀，然后是在《摩西和一神论》中对伟人的谋杀。参见雅克·拉康，《研讨班7：精神分析的伦理学》:，纽约：诺顿，1992,81,174。
Recall Lacan's position in Seminar VII, in which, in a Hegelian manner, the word is understood as a murder of the Thing, and the signifier a mortification of jouissance. The topic is there developed in reference to the Freudian theme of the murder of the primordial father in Totem and Taboo and then the murder of the Great Man in Moses and Monotheism. See J. Lacan, Seminar, Book VII, The Ethics of Psychoanalysis, New York: Norton, 1992, 81, 174.
