路易·阿尔都塞，《保卫马克思》，巴黎：马斯佩罗，1965年，235-6。正如阿尔都塞所说，马克思抛弃了将人的类本质作为政治经济学（批判）的基础。拉康通过引入无意识主体来坚持阿尔都塞的路线，因此提出了一种反人文主义理论的精神分析版本，但这却偏离了阿尔都塞，因为后者拒绝这个主体的概念。
L. Althusser, Pour Marx, Paris: Maspero, 1965, 235-6. According to Althusser, Marx abolished the category of man'as the main theoretical ground of (the critique of)political economy. Lacan pursued this Althusserian line by introducing the subject ofthe unconscious, thereby proposing a psychoanalytic version of theoretical anti-humanism, yet one which moved away from Althusser, who rejected the notion of the subject.
