这里我解释了拉康所描述的假设：“我的假设是，受无意识影响的个体与构成我所说的能指主体的个体是一样的。”（拉康《再来一次》，142页，翻译已修改）
I am here paraphrasing what Lacan describes as his hypothesis: ‘My hypothesis is that the individual who is affected by the unconscious is the same as the one that constitutes what I call the subject of a signifier’ (Lacan, Encore, 142, translation modified).
