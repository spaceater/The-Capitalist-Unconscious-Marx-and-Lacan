人们有时会听到人们对马克思在《资本论》中没有使用“无产阶级”一词感到惊讶。他不需要这样做，因为“劳动力”、“人口过剩”和“工业后备军”都指涉了同样的主观立场。
One can sometimes hear astonishment over the fact that Marx does not use the term proletariat’ or ‘proletarian’ in Capital. He does not need to because ‘labour-power’, ‘surplus population’ and ‘industrial reserve army’ designate the very same subjective position.
