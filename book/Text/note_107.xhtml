“无产阶级和资本主义阶级同样受资本的约束，但前者更进一步：资本可以脱离于资本家而存在，但无法脱离于创造价值的劳动而存在。”莫伊舍·普殊同，《时间、劳动与社会统治》，剑桥：剑桥大学出版社，2003年，357年。
‘Both the proletariat and the capitalist class are bound to capital, but the former is more so: capital conceivably could exist without capitalists, but it could not exist without value-creating labor.’ M. Postone, Time, Labor, and Social Domination, Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2003, 357.
