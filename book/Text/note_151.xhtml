我们可以发现，这三个功能和三个拉康式的话语完美重叠：知识的生产对应于癔症话语，其中主体向主人准确地说出它想知道的欲望；意义的生产指向大学话语，其中知识支撑起了现实的整体性；最后秩序的生产作为一种基本的社会联结，与主人话语密切相关。
We can note in passing that the three functions perfectly overlap with the three Lacanian discourses: production of knowledge pertains to the hysterics discourse, where the subject addresses the master precisely with the desire to know; production of meaning points to the university discourse, where knowledge supports the totalisation of reality; and finally the production of order is intimately related to the master’s discourse as the fundamental social link.
