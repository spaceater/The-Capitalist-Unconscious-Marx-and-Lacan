Marcuse's Eros and Civilization (Boston: Beacon Press, 1966 [1955]) remains the most accomplished document of this direction. For a critical account of Marcuse's reading of Freud, see J. Laplanche, Le primat de lautre en psychanalyse, Paris: Flammarion, 1997, 59-88.
