拉康将把这节弗洛伊德式的教训翻译成一般公理：欲望是大他者的欲望（拉康，《他者选集》，207页）。这种说法的意义是双重的：“欲望是大他者的欲望”强调异化的维度和欲望的加倍，主体和大他者的关系，没有欲望的主体和欲望铭刻于大他者身上而且属于大他者；但公理也意味着“欲望着”，似乎针对一个具体对象的欲望，是“大他者的欲望”，实际上是针对剩余客体的欲望。
Lacan will translate this Freudian lesson in the general axiom: le désir de est le désir de l’Autre (Lacan, Autres écrits, 207). The meaning of this claim is double: ‘desire is desire of the Other’ accentuates the dimension of alienation and places the doubling on the level of desire at the forefront, the relation between the subject and the Other, the fact that there is no subject of desire and that desire is inscribed in the Other and belongs to the Other; but the axiom also means that ’desire of’, desire that seems to aim at a concrete object, is ‘desire of the Other’, desire that actually aims at the surplus-object.
